Why CodeHerWay Exists

There's no shortage of coding tutorials on the internet. But most of them weren't written with you in mind.

They assume you already know the jargon. They skip the "why" and jump to the "how." They make you feel like you're behind before you've even started.

CodeHerWay was built to be different. Every tutorial, every series, every Dev_Fession on this site is written the way we wish someone had explained it to us — clearly, patiently, and without gatekeeping.

"If this place makes even one woman feel less alone in tech, it's worth every late night and every console.log."

What You'll Find Here

81 free tutorials across HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — structured into complete series that build on each other. Plus Dev_Fessions: honest, sometimes embarrassing stories from the coding trenches that remind you every developer started exactly where you are.

No paywalls. No upsells. No "subscribe to unlock." Just code — explained clearly, by someone who's been there.

Who Built This

CodeHerWay was built by Jenna Zawaski — a developer, educator, and professional bug whisperer based in Chicago, IL. She started this project because she believes the tech industry gets better when more women have a seat at the table — and the skills to build their own.
